BMI Guide

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement of a person’s weight with respect to his or her height.
It is more of an indicator than a direct measurement of a person’s total body fat. The BMI (Body Mass Index) is used to quickly and simply determine a
person’s weight in regard to their height. BMI determine underweight, normal, overweight or obese.
